    • About God Saves Apostolic Church

    God Saves Apostolic Church is a Christ-centered, Spirit-led community rooted in the Apostolic faith. Our mission is to spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture spiritual growth, and build strong relationships within our congregation and the broader community.

    Our Vision

    To be a vibrant and unified body of believers that transforms lives through worship, fellowship, discipleship, and service. We seek to be a beacon of hope and healing in our community, living out the teachings of Christ in all we do.

    What We Believe

    • We believe in one God, manifest in Jesus Christ.
    • We uphold the Bible as the inspired Word of God.
    • We teach repentance, baptism in Jesus' name, and the infilling of the Holy Spirit (Acts 2:38).
    • We strive to live holy, Spirit-led lives in obedience to God's Word.
